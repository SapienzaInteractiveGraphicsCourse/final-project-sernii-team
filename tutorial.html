Gameplay

  • Avoid the spikeball and collect as many stars as possible in order to reach the highest score possible
  • At the beginning you start with three lives, every time you get hit by a spikeball you take a step back and lose one heart.
  • Collect hearts in order to restore lives
  • When a spikeball hit you, you acquire temporary invulnerability
  • When you become invulnerable, you cannot lose life but neither collect hearts or stars

Commands

  • Press ESC to pause the game
  • Press SPACE to jump
  • Press 1,2 or 3 to change the camera position
  • Press 4 to lock/unlock the camera. Once unlocked u can control it by pressing the left click and moving it
  • Press A or ← to dash left
  • Press D or → to dash right
